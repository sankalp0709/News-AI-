## Production Notes

### Recording Tips:
1. **Use screen recording software**: OBS Studio, Loom, or QuickTime
2. **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
3. **Frame rate**: 30fps minimum
4. **Audio**: Clear voiceover or add captions
5. **Browser**: Use Chrome with DevTools closed
6. **Zoom**: 100% browser zoom

### Editing Tips:
1. **Add transitions** between scenes
2. **Highlight important elements** with zoom or cursor effects
3. **Add text overlays** for key features
4. **Keep it concise** - aim for 1-2 minutes
5. **Add background music** (optional, keep it subtle)

### Upload:
1. **YouTube**: Upload as unlisted or public
2. **Cloud Storage**: Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.
3. **Add link to README.md** after upload
